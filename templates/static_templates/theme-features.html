About OpenEdX

VeritasU leverages OpenEdX, to deliver great online courses. Open-EdX is the leading open source online learning platform, which powers MOOCs (massive open online courses) from Harvard, MIT, and other EdX partners. OpenEdX features include:

  • Studio online course creation
  • Online course LMS
  • Extensibility via Xblock plug-ins and LTI integrations
  • Leading-edge assessment capabilities
